WANTED: VILLIANS

HOW TO

GET ONE

Your own Wanted: Villian.

Every Wanted: Villian is a handmade 1/1 — no two are the same. There are two ways to get one:

Option 1 — Burn & customise

  1. Burn 10 Villains from either of our other two collections — Villains by Nev. or Ape Villains by Nev..
  2. Message Nev on X — @byNeviras — and let him know you have burned.
  3. Nev keeps a burn list and works through it in order. If you buy Villains now to burn, you join the queue — you may need to wait your turn.
  4. Nev will talk with you about traits and details for your character. Browse the prototype character roster for inspiration.
  5. Your custom 1/1 Wanted: Villian is hand-drawn by @iambennnn_ and sent to you.

Option 2 — Buy direct

Prefer to skip the burn? Some 1/1 listings will be available to buy straight from the Wanted: Villains collection on OpenSea. Check there for what is live.

Either way — burn or buy — each Villian is handmade and unique. Welcome to the wanted list.
