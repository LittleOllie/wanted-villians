About The Project

"Villains by Nev." came to life 2024 and is a unique collection of pixel art NFTs, inspired by piv_piv & punk world culture.

From the beginning there were 2121 Villains. One, a mighty Zombie King, was burned as a gimmick - and later on resurrected in our honorary collection, the VIP Villains. Since the Zombie King wants more victims to follow his path, holders of any Villain collection can choose to burn Villains and let them be rescued among the VIPs too. The more you burn, the more customized VIP will show up...

We have one additional collection on Base: Winter Villains. Although these (originally) 1111 mischievous Villains have a winter aura, they are active all year round.

2025, we stepped into Apechain to expand our community. Check out Ape Villains!

All ouf our collections will be cherished and play an important role in the world of Villains. Welcome!
